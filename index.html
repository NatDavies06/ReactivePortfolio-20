Some Small Talk

My name is Nathan Davis, and my journey has been quite unexpected. When I was young, my goal was to move out of Ohio and pursue a career in web development. However, life often has different plans. Despite still living in Ohio and working a warehouse job for eight years, I've been blessed with a wonderful partner and three children I couldn’t live without. Life also gave me a second chance. Recently, I completed the University of Minnesota's Coding Bootcamp, an achievement that has opened exciting doors to the future. This profile serves as a showcase of my journey—highlighting the projects I've developed through the bootcamp as well as those I've pursued independently. Each accomplishment reflects not only my skills but also my personality, determination, and passion for growth.
